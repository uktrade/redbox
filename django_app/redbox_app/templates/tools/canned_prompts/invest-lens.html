{{ tool.name }} combines Data Hub and external sources to help you build briefings, join up company and sector insights, and develop strong UK investment hooks

It pulls company data and interaction records from Data Hub, along with web search results and built-in investment documents. It also includes citations that link back to these original sources.

To get started and get the most out of the tool:

  • type the name of the company and any parameters (such as date range or geography) into the message box below
  • specify your target audience (for example, ministerial brief, internal meeting) or refine your search terms
  • add more context and additional information by uploading your own documents. For example, information in SharePoint or behind subscriptions
How the tool works

The tool will:

  • automatically search across companies, subsidiaries, and related entities in Data Hub
  • (where relevant) use its built-in investment documents and a web search to pull contextual information and relevant news
  • provide comprehensive research summaries or briefings in a format you choose, for example a ministerial briefing
  • give you clear citations linking back to original Data Hub records, online sources and documents
How to get more from this tool

You can get more from this tool by:

  • uploading any relevant contextual documents to the submission’s topic. Use the message box to ask Assist to consider them in its review
  • asking Assist to only consider specific criteria. For example, as you update your draft you can ask it to review it only for narrative flow
