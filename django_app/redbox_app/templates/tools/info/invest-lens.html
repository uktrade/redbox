{% extends "tools/base-tool-info.html" %} {% block tool_description %} The Invest Lens tool provides investment advisers and officials with a briefing through quick, comprehensive access to company information and DBT relationship data from Data Hub, searching the web and built-in investment documents. You can add more specific and additional information for the tool to use by uploading your own documents. {% endblock %} {% block tool_when_to_use %}
  • If you need to prepare a briefing for a meeting with a company at short notice.
  • If you want comprehensive company information from DBT and external investment sources.
  • If you need to retrieve investment project details or account management objectives by time period or geography.
  • If you're attending conferences or events and need quick access to multiple company relationships.
{% endblock %} {% block tool_how_to_use %}
  • specify the company of interest and any details you are interested in (such as date range or geography)
  • you can refine searches, and specify your target audience (e.g., ministerial brief, internal meeting)
  • the tool will automatically search across companies, subsidiaries, and related entities in Data Hub
  • where relevant, the tool will also use it’s built-in investment documents and a web search to pull contextual information and relevant news.
  • for a more holistic review and where information exists in sharepoint or behind subscriptions, you can upload additional documents to provide the tool with more context, such as previous briefings or notes
  • the tool will provide comprehensive summaries in the form of a ministerial briefing with citations linking back to original Data Hub records, online sources and documents
{% endblock %} {% block tool_document_guidance %}

note: you can upload additional documents to the chat, such as previous briefings, notes, or other company-related materials to enhance context.
