Invest Lens combines Data Hub and external sources to help you build briefings, join up company and sector insights, and develop strong UK investment hooks

It pulls company data and interaction records from Data Hub, along with web search results and built-in investment documents.

Invest Lens is designed mainly for investment advisers and officials

It can search across parent companies, subsidiaries, and related entities. This means users get a more complete picture without needing to look things up manually. It can also generate briefings in formats familiar to investment advisers.

{% endblock %} {% block tool_when_to_use %}
  • you need to prepare a briefing at short notice
  • you're conducting exploratory or early-stage research - it helps you bring together information on companies or sectors all in one place
  • you want to brainstorm or refine investment hooks using existing proposition documents
  • you need to retrieve investment project details or account management objectives by time period or geography
  • you're attending conferences or events and need quick access to multiple company relationships
{% endblock %} {% block tool_how_to_use %}
  • specify the company of interest and any parameters (such as date range or geography)
  • refine your search terms
  • specify your target audience (for example, ministerial brief, internal meeting)
  • add more context and additional information by uploading your own documents. For example, information in SharePoint or behind subscriptions
{% endblock %} {% block tool_additional_content %}

How the tool works

The tool will:

  • automatically search across companies, subsidiaries, and related entities in Data Hub
  • (where relevant) use its built-in investment documents and a web search to pull contextual information and relevant news
  • provide comprehensive research summaries or briefings in a format you choose, for example a ministerial briefing
  • give you clear citations linking back to original Data Hub records, online sources and documents
{% endblock %} {% block tool_document_guidance %}

It uses the following documents to build briefings:

  • Data Hub company records
  • briefing templates specific to investment contexts (maintained by admins)
  • built-in investment documents (maintained by admins)
