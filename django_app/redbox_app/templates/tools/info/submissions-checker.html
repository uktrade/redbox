{% extends "tools/base-tool-info.html" %} {% block tool_description %}

The Submissions Checker helps you assess whether your draft submission aligns with the 'Ministerial template guidance'. It reviews your submission against 7 criteria taken from that guidance.

For each criterion, you'll get:

  • a score
  • a short explanation
  • specific examples

Once all 7 parts are checked, you'll get an average score, summary and recommendations to improve your draft.

{% endblock %} {% block tool_when_to_use %}
  • you're reviewing a submission and want a quick overview of its strengths and weaknesses
  • you want to check that your first draft is on the right track
  • you want a final sense check before submitting
  • you want detailed feedback on a draft
{% endblock %} {% block tool_how_to_use %}
  • go to the submission checker tool (from the menu or green button below) and upload your submission draft in the chat box
  • if you don't enter a prompt with your upload, the tool will review your submission against 7 key criteria, score it and give you an explanation
  • if you want the review to focus on a particular area, type a prompt before clicking send
  • for a more holistic review, you can upload additional documents to provide more context
{% endblock %} {% block tool_document_guidance_footer %}

note: you can upload additional documents to the chat, such as important ministerial context or prior example submissions.
