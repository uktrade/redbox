What you can do with Redbox

Upload

Easily upload a wide range of document formats into your personal Redbox. Ask Redbox for instant summaries of individual documents or combine multiple documents to discover connections across your content.

Chat

Chat with Redbox and ask questions about your documents to uncover insights using AI. Redbox uses the latest available model which includes general knowledge up to October 2024. When needed, Redbox can search gov.uk and Wikipedia to provide you with the most relevant information. It cannot access websites outside of these.

Secure

Redbox is built in our secure and private cloud which enables you to upload, up to, and including, {{ security }} documents in your Redbox. Documents are uploaded to your personal Redbox which only you can see.

Summarise

Use Redbox to summarise long reports, memos, policies and legislative material to extract key points and identify underlying themes.

Analyse

Use Redbox to analyse documents and provide different perspective on them.
